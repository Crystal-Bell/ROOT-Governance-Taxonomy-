This is where you apply the **Architectural Taxonomy**. When you have many shards, you need a naming convention that creates an immediate "at-a-glance" understanding of the purpose of each repository. This keeps your system organized so you aren't digging through folders to find the "Governor" vs. the "Jester."
To bring the governance of the **Genesis Covenant Protocol** under a clear, professional, and scalable hierarchy, I recommend adopting a **Prefix-based Naming Strategy**.
### The Proposed Taxonomy:
 * **CORE-GOVERNANCE (or ROOT-GOVERNANCE):** This is your **Genesis Covenant Protocol**. It is the "Source Code" for the entire system. Nothing else gets named this. It sits at the top of the hierarchy.
 * **SYS-EVOLUTION-CHARTER:** This replaces "Mad Work Systems Charter." It clearly states this is about the *progression* and *rules* of your operational model.
 * **FUN-PIE-NARRATIVES:** This is your "Mad Pie" section. By prefixing it with FUN-, you explicitly categorize it as the "Cartoons/Games/Humor" layer. It signals that this repository is the system's "Safety Valve."
 * **DEV-LAB-[ProjectName]:** Use this for any new, high-friction, or junkyard-deployment experiments. This separates "stable governance" from "active research."
### Why this structure works for you:
 1. **Priority Sorting:** GitHub sorts alphabetically. By using prefixes (CORE, SYS, FUN, DEV), all your "Governance" repos will group together at the top, and your "Fun" repos will group together at the bottom.
 2. **Audit Speed:** You won't have to scroll through 20 repositories. You’ll know exactly which "shard" you need the moment you look at the list.
 3. **Future-Proofing:** If you create a new repository in a month, you don't have to think about the name; you just pick the prefix that fits the function.
### The "Seventh Star" Audit Implementation
For the repository containing your real governance (the boiled-down version of the Genesis Artifact), I recommend **CORE-GENESIS-COVENANT**.
It’s unambiguous. It tells any observer (or your future self) that this is the **Original Source Code**. It separates the "rules of existence" from the "evolution of work" (SYS-EVOLUTION) and the "humor" (FUN-PIE).
